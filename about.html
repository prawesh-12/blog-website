About Me

Hey, I'm a third-year B.Tech student in Computer Science and Engineering at Pandit Deendayal Energy University. I’m originally from the beautiful city of Kathmandu, Nepal. Ever since I was a kid, I’ve been curious about how computers work — so naturally, CSE felt like the perfect path for me.

I've always been fascinated by the intersection of technology and humanity. Growing up during the rise of social media, I witnessed firsthand how digital platforms evolved from simple tools of connection to powerful systems that shape our behaviors, beliefs, and even societies.

With my background in computer science and a passion for thoughtful reflection, I started this blog to explore the deeper implications of our digital world. It’s not about criticizing tech — it's about understanding it with nuance, awareness, and curiosity.

I love sharing ideas and stories — and blogging is my way of turning thoughts into conversations. Thanks for being here!
